De La Salle Hall is the most iconic dormitory hall in St. Mary's College of California.

It is a first year dormitory, that also doubles as a public meeting space on the first floor.

This premiere first year residence hall has...

  • Hagerty Lounge, a large open space great for events
  • CWAC, a school center there for your every writing need
  • An awesome residential team

We hope you consider joining De La Salle as your first hall!
