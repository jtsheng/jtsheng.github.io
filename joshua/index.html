Student Disability Services (SDS)

What I Do


This website describes my on-campus job. I am a student worker at SDS. I work on processing books for students with accommodations. This includes:

  • Processing student receipts for class textbooks.
  • Making sure books are in accessible formats, such as ensuring the books work with screen readers. This can be difficult if the quality of the book or scan is poor.
  • Captioning videos and describing images.

The software I use includes:

  1. Adobe Acrobat
    1. For adding bookmarks to PDFs.
    2. OCR for recognizing text in books without text already.
  2. Kurzweil 3000
    1. Screen reader software for students. I also use the software to upload books.
    2. Also used for zoning of the books to make sure they are read in the correct order.
  3. Microsoft Word
    1. For adding headings to documents and formatting text.
  4. Adobe Premiere
    1. For captioning videos.
