Claeys North and Claeys South will be undergoing faciltlies upgrades during the summer. Buildings in the upgrade will be offline during this time.

Second-year suite-style living near central campus.

History of the Building

Dedicated July 22, 1984 after Linus Claeys '32 (1909-1987) and his first wife, Edna. The site used to be the old rifle range for the Navy. Linus Claeys also has a section of Interstate 80 named for him.

Building Layout

Suites accommodate 6 residents, with 2 doubles and 2 singles. Each half of the suite has a sink and common space, and each side is connected with a shared shower and toilet. Each room includes a vanity dresser and lockable closet (two closets in double rooms). Each room feature a heater.

Building Amenities

  • Lounge with TV, kitchen area (oven, fridge, and sink) on each floor
  • Laundry with two washers and two dryers on each floor
  • Outdoor basketball court and barbeque
  • Independent heating in each room
  • Bike rack parking in front of building
  • Monthly ABM suite cleaning
