Last Season:

Last season was an epic one for the Gaels. We held a 12-2-4 record having the most wins since 2001, as well as tying program record with 6 straight shutouts. The Gaels placed second in the WCC and finished the season ranked 42 in the nation. Head coach Theresa Romagnolo was named the WCC coach of the year, along with player Kate Plachy being named WCC goalkeeper of the year. We set a program record with 7 WCC victories and recorded 12 shutouts - the second most in program history.

Our team values include:

  • Competitive - Every player is expected to treat every practice as if it is a game.
  • Resilience - Gaels rise up through hard challenges and overcome them.
  • Unity - Cultivate a good team culture that lifts those around you.
