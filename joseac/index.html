Extra

More resources related to the Department also include the Marie Curie STEM Center, where tutoring and workshops are offered to help student success across the STEM field at SMC, including for Math & CS students. The Center is located at Assumption Hall at the 2nd floor. A map is shown here:

The college also offers the Brother Dominic Barry Mathematics Scholarship for new student applicants who are accepted into the Department. To win the scholarship, students must be one of the two top scorers in the annual Brother Dominic Barry Math Problem Solving Competition, requiring no prerequisite knowledge other than high school math. It awards awarded $5,000 annually, and is renewable for four years.
