SMC Climbing Wall Information

The Saint Mary's College of California climbing wall is located at the Joseph Alioto Recreation Center. Completed in 2015, it features two auto-belay lanes and 3 top-rope lanes. Members must complete the belay class/assessment before climbing (top-rope).

Equipment Rental: all equipment is available to check out for recreation center members

  • Harness
  • Climbing shoes
  • Belay device
  • Carabiner
