"Exploring faith while building a community to last"

InterVarsity is working on Saint Marys campus to make campus as it is in heaven. We work to study the Bible and provide spaces for people to express their faith through musical worship and scripture studies. We also host community events such as game nights or Supper club which has one provide places to play and another to eat.
