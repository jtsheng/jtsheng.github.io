Saint Mary's College Men's Club Soccer

Team information

Saint Mary's Men's Club Soccer team is a competitive soccer team that allows students who are interested in soccer and love a challenge to play against other schools. They currently play in the Division 2 bracket of the West Coast Soccer Association. Some teams they play against include Sacramento State, Sonoma State, Northeastern University, UC Davis, and the University of San Francisco, and more! Practices take place on Tuesday nights from 6–8, Wednesday nights from 7–9, and Thursday nights from 7:30–9:30. Games are usually on Sunday afternoon.
