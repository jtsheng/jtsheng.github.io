About Club Tennis

Club Tennis is an opportunity for students to exercise their muscles, social skills, and teamwork. A place for all levels, beginner to advanced. Filled with team bonding and tournaments. We travel all around the bay to play other schools in team vs team tournaments. Tennis is a life sport, either continue or start your journey with us.
