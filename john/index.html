Computer Science

At Saint Mary’s College of California, a degree in Computer Science builds strong foundations in programming, algorithms, and other important skills; preparing students to build computer science solutions across industries.

The curriculum is designed to incorporate the ethics and philosophy of computer scientists to give students a well-rounded education and the capability to thrive anywhere in the field.

  • Tech Ethics
  • Web Programming
  • Analysis of Algorithms

Topics highlighted in Computer Science

The courses are kept up to date with the newest technologies and areas of interest, most recently with courses on AI.

Programming Languages

We teach Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Algorithms

Graphing, Sorting, and Storing algorithms are all taught.

Ethics

Knowing how to use the teachings given in the Computer Science major is important to prevent misuse of technology.
